Our Story

Who We Are

We're Sam and Nick Brusilow, founders of Westside Earthworks, and all-American grading, leveling, and site preparation business right here in Michigan. Proudly family owned and operated, we're dedicated to providing top-quality site solutions.

Our Purpose

We believe in waking up early, working hard, staying organized, and responding quickly. It all started with an observation. "It seems like everyone needs a contractor nowadays, and new construction is popping up everywhere you turn... But try to get something done and you're stuck waiting six months!" We make it our goal to wake up each day and be better than your average contractor. We want you to be so satisfied with our work and service that you come back each and every time you need land preparation work done right.
